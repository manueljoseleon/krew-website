Built and shipped the KREW landing page.

**What's in it:**
- **Sticky nav** with the geometric K logo (3 straight-cut lines) + wordmark
- **Hero** — headline left, tilted phone right showing the full **Krew Passport** (header, avatar PC, 4 certs with green/amber states, scanable QR with daily expiry)
- **Problem** — 3 cards with monospace meta footers ("6–8 h", "3–5 días")
- **Platform** — 5 vertical tabs that auto-rotate; each swaps a real-looking mockup in the canvas: Passport phone, matching dashboard with % bars, traffic-light compliance panel, dotación table, mobile payments app
- **How it works** — animated 3-step horizontal stepper with mock CLI lines
- **Audience** — two-column block, dark CTA for mineras / blue CTA for trabajadores
- **Metrics** — 4 large blue stats on light-grey
- **Contact** — validated form with submit/success state
- **Footer** with logo, tagline, LinkedIn, copyright

**Aesthetic notes:**
- White background, true black ink, blue #1A6FFF used heavily for highlights/CTAs, yellow #FFB800 only as a subtle hero glow accent
- Space Grotesk 700 for headings, Inter for body, JetBrains Mono for meta/labels (used sparingly to feel like a tooling-aware product, not a corporate site)
- Generous 96px section padding, 16–20px radii, scroll-reveal fades, hover lifts
- All mockup data is fictional but plausible (real Chilean mining context: El Teniente, Los Bronces, OS-10, Sence, ACHS, AFP, turnos 7×7)Ending my turn.
